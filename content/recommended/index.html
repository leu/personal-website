Current philosophy

I think it's important to go out of your way to try new ways of doing things whenever you can. This is how you discover how to be better than you are now. I suppose this links strongly to the scientific method, but in the form of life advice.

On top of that, pay close attention to what you intuitively think of a decision. Sometimes you already intuitively know if something will lead to worse outcomes, but don't know any other way of doing things. It may be worth thinking of some new idea which you feel roughly neutral about, and trying it out. It can be something small.
